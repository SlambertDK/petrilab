Research foundation · the theory we lean on

MODES · open-ended evolution axes

?MODES (Dolson et al. 2019) measures open-ended evolution along four axes instead of one score. We watch them together to tell real accumulation from mere oscillation.

Falsification contract

?Three independent tests that must ALL pass for us to claim open-ended evolution. Decoupled from the novelty metric so the system can't confirm its own definition. —

The Gardener · self-improving loop

?An autonomous experimenter. It forms a hypothesis, tunes ONE ratio (never rules/DNA), measures the effect, keeps or reverts, and learns which knobs help — biasing future hypotheses toward them.
